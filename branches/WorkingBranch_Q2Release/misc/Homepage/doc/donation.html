Donations

The Iometer project is run on a volunteer-basis and therefore is in first hand always looking for new people to get involved [more] in terms of writing code or documentations, doing testing etc.

To get this job done and to ensure that we are able to run and support a wide range of Operating System/Processor combinations, we need your support for our mission. We kindly ask you for donating hard- and software (for porting, testing and prebuild binary delivery), services (like webspace/traffic) or money (translates into the former ones) to help us providing you with a powerful tool for you platform.
