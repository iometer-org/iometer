IOMETER can not be here without efforts and support from many individuals and 
companies. This is at least a partial credits file to list people and companies 
that have contributed to the IOMETER project. It borrows the format used in 
Linux kernel project and is sorted by last name.

The fields are: 
	Name (N),
	Email (E),
	Company (C),
	Web-address (W),
	Description (D)

Note: This list is far from complete. Especially special thanks go to our end
	users. And we are sorry that we can not list your names here.
	
	Please notify Daniel or Ming if there is anything missed or wrong. 
	Thank you for your long time support on IOMETER!
